Introduction to Sologamy

What is it?

A popular concept that encourages you to adopt self-marriage in an effort to look after the one person you will always be in a long-term relationship with â€“ yourself.

Be Happy

Engaging in self-love will help you blossom into a beautiful, happy, fully alive person who accepts every part of themselves and others.

Promise Vows

You can use promise vows to make a promise to yourself and/or use them as part of your sologamy ceremony. Our club shows you how.

Self-Love

Because in learning to love yourself and be kind to yourself it makes it easier for you to spread that love and compassion to others.

Be Committed

Being committed to loving yourself will support you in developing a loving, long-term relationship with someone else.

Ceremony

Invite people along to witness your commitment to yourself and celebrate your big day or keep it private within your own home. Your party. Your choice.

Join Our Tribe
